# Stay Safe Online Checklist

## 🔒 General Safety Tips
- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Verify information before sharing online
- Keep your apps and devices updated
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for sensitive tasks
- Enable two-factor authentication

## 💰 UPI / Financial Safety
- Never share your UPI PIN with anyone
- Do not approve unknown payment requests
- Always verify the receiver before sending money

## 🚨 Reporting Cybercrime
- Report incidents at: https://cybercrime.gov.in
- Call Cybercrime Helpline: 1930
